#### Overview

**Transaction Inrerpreter** is a tool to extract and interpret large-scale cryptocurrency transactions (whale movements). The platform combines multiple blockchain data sources with AI-powered analysis to provide actionable insights for traders, analysts, and crypto enthusiasts.

#### Primary Users
- **Crypto Traders**: Track whale movements to identify market trends and trading opportunities
- **DeFi Analysts**: Monitor large token transfers and their impact on token prices
- **Crypto Researchers**: Study whale behavior patterns and market dynamics
- **Portfolio Managers**: Get alerts on significant market movements

#### Key Use Cases
1. **Whale Transaction Monitoring**: Track large transfers across major tokens
2. **Price Impact Analysis**: Measure how whale transactions affect token prices
3. **Market Intelligence**: Generate AI-powered insights from transaction data
4. **Risk Assessment**: Identify potential market manipulation or significant movements

### Business Value Proposition

#### For Traders
- **Early Warning System**: Identify significant market movements before they impact prices
- **Risk Management**: Track whale movements that could affect portfolio positions
- **Market Intelligence**: Understand the context behind large transactions

#### For Analysts
- **Data-Driven Insights**: Comprehensive transaction analysis with multiple data sources
- **Research Tools**: Advanced analytics for studying market behavior
- **Reporting**: Automated generation of professional analysis reports

#### For Institutions
- **Market Surveillance**: Monitor for potential market manipulation
- **Compliance**: Track large transactions for regulatory reporting
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify potential market risks from whale movements
